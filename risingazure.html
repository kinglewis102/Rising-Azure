Welcome to Rising Azure

Rising Azure is a site designed to help new players to the Blazblue series set goals for themselves and improve at the game. Here, you can divide your goals into individual sections: Offense, Defense, Neutral, and Character Matchups. You can upload your matches here and divide your goals into those 4 sections. This site is primarly focused on Blazblue Centralfiction and not Blazblue Cross Tag Battle.

Relentlessly pressure your opponnent and make them too afraid to press any buttons. A good offense is the cornerstone of pushing your advantage in the max. Perhaps yours could use a few changes her in there? Put goals in this section to become an even fiercer opponent.

Be an impenetrable wall that can withstand any form of attack. Learning how to play on the defense in a disadvantageous posistion is what will allow you to stay safe and find an oportunity to go on the offense. Maybe your defense is a little to easy to break? Put goals here to further strengthen your guard.
